## 1️⃣ Integration Workspace Glass ⭐ NEW!

### **The Premium Choice**

**Design Philosophy:**
- Glassmorphism meets workflow builder
- Light, airy, and elegant
- Single unified color scheme (indigo)
- Professional yet playful

### **Key Features:**
✨ **Frosted Glass Cards** - Backdrop blur effects
🎨 **Light Pastels** - Blue, indigo, purple, pink, cyan
💎 **Unified Indigo** - All buttons use same color
🌊 **Smooth Animations** - 300ms transitions
📐 **Dot Grid** - Light indigo pattern
🔗 **Glass Lines** - Transparent connections

### **Color Scheme:**
```
Background: Gradient (blue-50 → indigo-50 → purple-50)
Cards: White 40% opacity + blur
Buttons: Indigo-500/90 (ALL buttons)
Text: Gray-800 (dark for contrast)
Accents: Indigo-600
```

### **Perfect For:**
- 🎨 **Design-forward brands**
- 💼 **Premium SaaS products**
- ✨ **Modern workflow tools**
- 🚀 **Startup presentations**
- 💎 **Luxury applications**

### **Pros:**
- ✅ Visually stunning
- ✅ Consistent button design
- ✅ Modern and trendy
- ✅ High-end feel
- ✅ Light and airy

### **Cons:**
- ⚠️ Requires good hardware for blur
- ⚠️ May feel "too light" for some
- ⚠️ Single color can limit expression

### **Use When:**
You want to **impress** with a modern, elegant interface that feels premium and professional.
