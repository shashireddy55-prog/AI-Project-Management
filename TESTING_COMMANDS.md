## 💡 Tips for Testing

1. **Start Simple**: Try basic commands first, then move to complex ones
2. **Mix It Up**: Test different workspace types (Kanban, Scrum, Business, Test)
3. **Check Details**: Verify that epics, stories, tasks, and sprints are created properly
4. **Test Editing**: After creation, try editing and updating workspaces
5. **Try AI Assistant**: Use the floating AI button to ask questions about your workspace
6. **Export Data**: Test the export functionality to see the complete structure
7. **Import Data**: Try importing from Jira or other tools
