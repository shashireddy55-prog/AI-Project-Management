# 🤖 AI-Powered Sprint Planning - Test Commands Guide

## Overview
The AI-Powered Sprint Planning feature in Projify AI helps you automatically generate sprint goals and intelligently select stories based on your priorities and constraints.

---

## 📋 How to Access

1. Navigate to **Sprints** tab in your workspace
2. Click **"+ Create Sprint"** button
3. Enable **"AI-Powered Sprint Planning"** checkbox
4. Enter an AI prompt in the text area
5. Click **"Generate Sprint Plan"** button

## 💡 Tips for Effective AI Prompts

### ✅ **DO:**
- Be specific about your goals and priorities
- Mention story point capacity or team size
- Specify feature areas or domains
- Include priority levels (high, medium, low)
- Mention any constraints (dependencies, deadlines)

### ❌ **DON'T:**
- Use vague prompts like "plan a sprint"
- Omit context about team capacity
- Ignore existing backlog priorities
- Request contradictory objectives
- Forget to mention critical constraints

When you click "Generate Sprint Plan", the AI will:

1. ✅ **Generate Sprint Goal** - A clear, concise sprint objective

3. ✅ **Recommend Capacity** - Suggest optimal story point capacity
4. ✅ **Prioritize Work** - Order stories by business value and dependencies

## 🧪 Quick Test Workflow

```
1. Go to Sprints tab
2. Click "+ Create Sprint"
3. Enable "AI-Powered Sprint Planning"
4. Paste a sample command (e.g., "Focus on user authentication...")
5. Click "Generate Sprint Plan"
6. Wait for AI to populate:
   - Sprint Goal
   - Selected Stories (checkboxes)
   - Recommended Capacity
7. Review and adjust as needed
8. Fill remaining fields (dates, name)
9. Click "Create Sprint"
```
