# 🚀 FlowForge AI - Quick Start Guide

## Welcome to FlowForge!

FlowForge is your AI-powered project manager that transforms ideas into structured workspaces instantly.

---

## 📋 5-Minute Quick Start

### 1️⃣ **Login**
- Use your email and password
- First time? Click "Sign Up" tab

### 2️⃣ **Create Your First Workspace**
Click the **"Commands"** button in the header or use the AI input box with one of these commands:

**Try This:**
```
Build a mobile banking app with 8 engineers for 6 months
```

### 3️⃣ **Watch AI Magic**
FlowForge AI will automatically create:
- ✅ Complete workspace structure
- ✅ Project with epics and stories
- ✅ Tasks with details and estimates
- ✅ Sprint planning
- ✅ Team assignments

### 4️⃣ **Explore Your Workspace**
- Click on your new workspace card
- See the Kanban board with all tasks
- Drag & drop tasks between columns
- View reports and analytics

## 💡 Pro Tips

### Make Commands More Specific
❌ **Too Simple:** "Create a project"
✅ **Better:** "Build a fintech app with React, Node.js, 6 engineers, 3-month timeline"

### Include Key Details
- **Team size**: "with 8 engineers"
- **Timeline**: "for 6 months"
- **Technologies**: "using React and Node.js"
- **Features**: "with authentication and payment processing"

## 🛠️ Common Tasks

### Create a Story
1. Open workspace Kanban board
2. Click "Add Story" button
3. Fill in title, description, epic
4. Click "Create"

### Add Tasks to Story
1. Click on a story card
2. Scroll to "Tasks" section
3. Click "Add Task"
4. Fill in details and save

### Move Tasks
- Simply **drag and drop** tasks between TODO, IN PROGRESS, and DONE columns

### Generate AI Content
1. Click floating AI button (bottom right)
2. Ask: "Add 3 more stories to Epic 1"
3. AI creates them instantly

### View Reports
1. Click workspace menu (⋮)
2. Select "View Reports"
3. Explore charts and insights
