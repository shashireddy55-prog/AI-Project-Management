# ⚡ Quick Start - Integration Workspace Glass

## 🚀 Get Started in 30 Seconds

### **1. Login**
Default credentials are in the app. Just click "Sign In".

### **2. You'll See:**
A beautiful **glassmorphism canvas** with light colors!

### **3. Try These:**

#### **Drag Integration Cards**
- Click and drag any integration card
- Move it anywhere on the canvas
- See smooth animations

#### **Add More Integrations**
- Scroll to the bottom panel
- Click any app to add it
- It appears on the canvas

#### **Remove Cards**
- Hover over any card
- Click the **indigo X button** in the corner
- Card disappears

#### **Collapse Panel**
- Click the **indigo maximize button** in panel header
- Panel slides down
- Click "Show Integrations" to restore
